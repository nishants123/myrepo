What is IndianGPT?
IndianGPT is an easy-to-use AI chat assistant designed for everyone in India.
Current Version: 1.0 (Understands only English.)
Upcoming: Our Pune-based team is working hard to add support for Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Bengali, and other Indian languages, plus many new features.

Whether you're a student, professional, or just curious, IndianGPT is your digital companion for learning, productivity, and fun.
Release Date: It will be announced soon.
About Us
IndianGPT is developed by a passionate team of AI engineers and linguists. Our mission is to make AI accessible, useful, and safe for every Indian. We believe in the power of technology to bridge gaps and empower people in their daily lives.
